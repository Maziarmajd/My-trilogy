Matrix Trilogy

The Matrix

Release Year: 1999

  • Directors: Lana Wachowski, Lilly Wachowski
  • Starring: Keanu Reeves as Thomas A. Anderson / Neo, Laurence Fishburne as Morpheus, Carrie-Anne Moss as Trinity, Hugo Weaving as Agent Smith, Joe Pantoliano as Cypher
  • Neo awakens to the Matrix's simulated reality. With the rebels, he fights the machines as "The One." Neo confronts Agent Smith and decides humanity's destiny. Through his extraordinary abilities, he shapes the Matrix's fate.

The Matrix Reloaded

Release Year: 2003

  • Directors: Lana Wachowski, Lilly Wachowski
  • Starring: Keanu Reeves as Thomas A. Anderson / Neo, Laurence Fishburne as Morpheus, Carrie-Anne Moss as Trinity, Hugo Weaving as Agent Smith, Jada Pinkett Smith as Niobe
  • Neo, Trinity, and Morpheus continue their fight against the machines in the Matrix. They seek the Architect to uncover the truth. Agent Smith evolves and poses a threat to both humans and machines. The film explores deeper philosophical themes.

The Matrix Revolutions

Release Year: 2003

  • Directors: Lana Wachowski, Lilly Wachowski
